Dr. Donna Okuda, Trainer

Dr. Donna Okuda is a Hawaii native, who earned her undergraduate degree at Stanford University, then attended the University of Hawaii John A Burns School of Medicine. She has been practicing Obstetrics and Gynecology for twenty-nine years integrating the best of western medicine, integrative medicine and motivational interviewing (coaching) to help people identify, determine and own their health and wellness solutions with the medical professional as collaborator. Donna is passionate about the potential that pro-active, involved citizens can have on their learning, work capacity and overall health and prosperity. She sees coaching as an opportunity to develop those skills for personal and professional success and lifelong contributions that create meaning, solve problems and help others achieve fulfilling results in their lives.
