Annette Watkins, Affiliate/Trainer

Annette Watkins, founder of Tabula Rasa Training and an Academic Coach with LifeBound, is dedicated to assisting young people transition from high school to post-secondary education. She has successfully established operations throughout Australia and Asia Pacific delivering programs, workshops and accredited courses to students, parents, teachers, academics and trainers. Annette is an academic, trainer, author and public speaker in the field of transition skills for young people into post-secondary education. This has taken her around the globe speaking with international experts in the field.
