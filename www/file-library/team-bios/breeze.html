Maureen Breeze, Senior VP

Maureen Breeze has been a featured speaker at many international and national conferences on the topics of student success, academic coaching, leadership, and critical and creative thinking including the ECIS Conference in Hamburg, Germany; the Tri-Association Conference in Monterrey, Mexico; and the Schools of the Future Conference in Honolulu, HI, Education First in Davos, Switzerland; and the International Thinking Conference in Bilbao, Spain.

In addition to speaking, she trains and certifies college faculty and administrators, K12 educators, tutors, advisors, and parents in academic coaching skills, and has facilitated trainings at conferences, universities and other institutions both nationally and internationally.

Ms. Breeze co-authored Critical and Creative Thinking for Teenagers and Leadership for Teenagers, and developed print and on-line curricula for a series of student success books. She graduated Phi Beta Kappa from Claremont McKenna College with a major in International Management and minor in Mathematics.
